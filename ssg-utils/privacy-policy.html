Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SSG Utils ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information when you authenticate via Roblox OAuth, including:

Your Roblox username and user ID The list of groups you joined and which rank you have in them

We do not collect passwords, emails, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

To verify your group rank and authorize access to the site

4. Data Storage and Security

We use session cookies to keep you logged in securely. No data is stored, at each server restart or if you close the tab, you'll be logged out.

5. Third-Party Services

Authentication and user data are handled via Roblox’s official OAuth API. We do not share or sell your data to any third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time by logging out or revoking SSG Utils access from your Roblox account’s authorized apps.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact @NovaXi.
