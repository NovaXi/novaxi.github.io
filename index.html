Who am I?

Hello!
Welcome to my site! I'm NovaXi, a Moderator and a Supervisor in the Signalling Department here at SCR! I'm in charge of the moderation of the SCR Official Game, making sure the server stays welcoming and safe for everyone. I also train and assess Trainee Signallers until they become Signallers!

Need help?

You have 2 ways to reach SCR's Supervisor and Moderator Team:

  • For urgent matters, feel free to ping an available Mod/SV in # general.
  • For non urgent matters, feel free to open a ticket using # ticket-info.
